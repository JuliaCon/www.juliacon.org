# Visa Information for JuliaCon Global 2026

JuliaCon Global 2026 takes place **August 10–15, 2026** at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz, Germany.
Germany is part of the Schengen Area, so depending on your nationality you may need to apply for a **Schengen visa** before travelling.

## Do You Need a Visa?

If you want to travel to Germany for a period of up to 90 days – e.g. for a visit, as a tourist, on business, or for medical treatment – you may require a Schengen visa.

An overview of the countries whose nationals require a Schengen visa for short stays or are exempt from the visa requirement for short stays can be found here:

- [Overview of visa requirements for short stays](https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/en/visa-service/231148-231148)

- [Schengen visa application](https://videx.diplo.de/videx/visum-erfassung/en/videx-kurzfristiger-aufenthalt)

More information on the Schengen visa is available on the website of the Federal Foreign Office.

- [Schengen visa](https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/de/service/visa-und-aufenthalt/schengenvisa?openAccordionId=item-2432304-0-panel)
- [FAQ visa](https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/en/visa-service/buergerservice/faq/606848-606848)


If you are unsure, contact the German embassy or consulate in your country.
